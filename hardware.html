Hardware Compatibility

Tested & Recommended Hackintosh Hardware

Home Guides Hardware Troubleshooting

CPU

  • Intel 4th Gen → 10th Gen (Best support)
  • Intel 11th Gen+ (Limited)
  • AMD Ryzen (Supported with patches)

GPU

  • Intel iGPU (HD 4600+)
  • AMD RX 460 → RX 6900
  • Not Supported: NVIDIA RTX / GTX 16xx+

Wi-Fi & Bluetooth

  • Broadcom BCM94360 / BCM94352
  • Fenvi T919 (Native)

Storage

  • NVMe SSD (Recommended)
  • SATA SSD
